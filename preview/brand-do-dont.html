Do
  • Clean white #FFFFFF as default background.
  • Tabular numerals on every metric.
  • One accent per slide. Scarcity = signal.
  • Title case eyebrows. Full-sentence titles with a period.
  • Bold numbers, not adjectives, in body copy.
Don't
  • No rounded corners on data surfaces. No shadows.
  • No gradients. No stock imagery. No emoji.
  • No coloured dividers. No warm cream bg.
  • No mixed weights inside a number.
  • Never centre body copy.
